Catch AI Translation Errors Before They Reach Clients

LinguaOps is an AI‑powered QA layer that catches morphology and terminology drift in Romanian machine translation. Built for freelancers and agencies who need to ship clean, client‑ready output — fast.

The Problem

AI translation is fast — but not trustworthy for Romanian.

Machine translation engines still butcher Romanian morphology: gender agreement, case endings, diacritics, and formal register. Post‑editing catches some of it — but not consistently. Every missed error is a client complaint waiting to happen.

The Vision

A QA layer that speaks Romanian natively.

LinguaOps scans your translated content before delivery — flagging morphology errors, inconsistent terminology, missing diacritics, and register mismatches. It learns your style guide and glossary so every output matches your standards.
